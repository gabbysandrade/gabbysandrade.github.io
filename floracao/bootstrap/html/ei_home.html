Photo: UN Photo/Evan Schneider

UN Secretary-General Ban Ki-moon and his wife traveled to New Zealand last week to tour Emergy's newly opened Gourmet Mokai greenhouse with Emergy CEO Mark Greenburg. Data Shows Early Success for Emergy Inc Arctic Wind Turbines
March 12, 2022
Photo: CC BY-NC-ND 2.0 by Statkraft

In 2018, Emergy Inc unveiled its plans to construct wind farms in five different locations in the Arctic Circle, with the aim of harnessing the strong winds provided by the Arctic polar vortex. Construction was delayed due to the COVID-19 pandemic, with the first two farms completed in May of 2021. Preliminary data now indicates the project will exceed expectations. "It's wonderful to see it come to fruition," says Mark Bingham, a conservationist and resource scientist at MIT. "Many companies make commitments on paper to fighting climate change, but very few have made it a reality in the way Emergy Incorporated has."

Emergy Inc Funds Agricultural Conservation Certification Testing for CIMMYT
March 16, 2022
Photo: CC BY-NC-SA 2.0 by CIMMYT

The International Maize and Wheat Improvement Center (CIMMYT) was able to conduct its first round of agricultural conservation certification tests, thanks to fundraising campaigns led by Emergy Inc. The campaigns provided means for starter kits and standards information to be made available to farming operations of all scales, as well as expanding the CIMMYT's team of testing facilitators.
